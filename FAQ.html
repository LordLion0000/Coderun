FAQ

What is CodeRun?

CodeRun is an IT marathon, the participants having to go through the checkpoints located at the partner companies. The event is dedicated to students that not only want to test, but also to develop programming skills, all while having an unique experience.

Where will CodeRun take place?

CodeRun will take place offline in Cluj-Napoca, but the exact location will be announced soon!

How will the competition take place?

The event is structured as follows: from the 18th to the 21st of april there will be a series of online trainings, held by representatives from the partner companies, meant to help the participants in the following challenges and the 22nd and 23rd are the competition days which will take place offline. Each day will consist of 3 tasks, each having a duration of 2 hours.

Any student, no matter the specialization they study!

CodeRun offers you the opportunity to directly communicate with companies that might bring you your future career.
